A COMPARISON OF STANDARD OF LIVING IN THE UK AND IT'S FORMER COLONIES

AIMS

Specifically, comparing standard of living in the UK to a selected former colonies to decipher factors associated with standard of living and how they vary across former colonies.

There are different ways of measuring standard of living. Factors to consider include HDI, GDP, Life expectancy, Government spending, literacy rate, unemployment rate, consumer spending, poverty rate and etc.

For most of the charts the same ten countries are used to maintain consistency. These countries were selected due to their differing characteristics such as GDP, HDI, and geographical location.
